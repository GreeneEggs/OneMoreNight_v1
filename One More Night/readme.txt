One More Night

Producer: Jacob S Greene
Designer: Jacob A Greene
Artist: Christopher Smith
Programer: Eric Hatfield

Instructions:

W: Move Up
A: Move Left
S: Move Down
D: Move Right

Up Arrow: Fire Weapon Upward
Left Arrow:Fire Weapon Left
Down Arrow:Fire Weapon Downward
Right Arrow:Fire Weapon Right

Drag and Drop towers using the left mouse button.
Drop selected tower onto the tower stands to place defences.

Cheat:

Restart Game: R
